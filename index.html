About Us

At Hela Bojun at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, we celebrate authentic Sri Lankan cuisine with a modern twist. Our mission is to serve wholesome dishes, beverages, sweets, and short eats made only with healthy, natural ingredients. Enjoy a welcoming space where tradition meets taste, and every meal feels like home.

Best Selling

  • Rice and Curry

    A wholesome plate of steamed rice served with a variety of flavorful Sri Lankan curries.

  • String Hoppers (Idiyappam)

    Soft, steamed rice flour noodles paired with coconut sambol and spicy curries.

  • Roti

    Freshly made flatbread, served with sambol or curry for a traditional taste.

  • Avocado Juice

    Healthy and refreshing avocado juice made from ripe avocados and natural sweeteners.

  • Ulundu Vadai

    Crispy lentil fritters made from black gram, seasoned with spices for a perfect snack.

  • Fruit Salad
