Who We Are

Rooted in Tradition,
Crafted with Care

At Hela Bojun Hala at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, we celebrate authentic Sri Lankan cuisine with a modern twist. Our mission is to serve wholesome dishes, beverages, sweets, and short eats made only with healthy, natural ingredients. Enjoy a welcoming space where tradition meets taste, and every meal feels like home.
