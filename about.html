Our Story

From a humble campus canteen to a beloved culinary landmark — discover how Hela Bojun grew to serve authentic Sri Lankan flavors to thousands of students every day.

Our Mission

To provide Sabaragamuwa University students and staff with nutritious, affordable, and delicious Sri Lankan meals made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients — nurturing both body and culture.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted campus dining experience in Sri Lanka, celebrating the richness of traditional cuisine while embracing modern standards of hygiene, sustainability, and service excellence.

Our Values

Authenticity, freshness, affordability, and community. We believe that a great meal brings people together, and we strive to create that warmth and connection with every dish we serve.

Our Journey

2019

The Beginning

Hela Bojun Hala opened its doors at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka as a small campus canteen with a simple vision: to serve authentic, home-style Sri Lankan food to students at affordable prices.

2020

Growing the Menu

Responding to student feedback, we expanded our menu to include a wider variety of traditional dishes, beverages, and short eats. Rice and Curry, String Hoppers, and Ulundu Vadai became instant favourites.

2021

Going Digital

We launched our digital menu display system, making it easier for students to browse the daily offerings. The Hela Bojun website debuted as a central hub for menu, news, and contact information.

2022

Award for Excellence

Hela Bojun received the "Best Campus Dining" recognition from Sabaragamuwa University, honoring our commitment to quality, hygiene, and authentic Sri Lankan cuisine. A proud milestone for our entire team.

2023

New Kitchen Facilities

Major upgrades to our kitchen infrastructure allowed us to serve more students faster while maintaining the highest standards of food safety and hygiene. Daily capacity grew to over 400 meals.

2024

Admin & Inventory System

We introduced a fully digital admin dashboard for real-time menu management and inventory tracking. This allowed us to reduce waste and ensure every item on the menu is always fresh and available.

2025

Serving 500+ Students Daily

Today, Hela Bojun proudly serves over 500 students and staff members every day. We continue to honour the essence of Sri Lankan culinary tradition while looking ahead to new ways of delighting our community.
